## Notes

- **Mode C**: each 6 h block reads `actual_weather[sample_hour = base_sample_hour + 6·block]`.
- **SR save %** is `(SR − baseline)/baseline · 100`. Negative is savings.
- **Luo − SR mt** is the SOG-lock cost.
- v_max = 25 kn fixed across all runs — high enough never to bind at any ETA in this matrix.
